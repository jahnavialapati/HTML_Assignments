Chocolate Chip Blondies Recipe

Required Ingredients are:

  • Flour
  • Brown Sugar
  • ½ cup chocolate chips
  • Nestle sweetened condensed milk
  • Egg
  • Vanilla
  • Butter
  • Nuts

Step by step procedure:

  1. Heat oven to 180 C or 350 F Stir together flour and brown sugar in medium bowl, cut in cold butter until crumbly.
  2. Stir in ½ cup chocolate chips, press mixture on bottom of ungreased baking dish. Bake for 15 minutes.
  3. Combine Nestle sweetened condensed milk,egg and vanilla in large bowl. Stir in remaining chocolate chips and nuts.
  4. Spread evenly over hot baked crust. Bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. Cool completely in pan on wire rack. Cut into squares.
